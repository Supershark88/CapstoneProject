Welcome to the Plankton to Pixels Website!

Plankton to Pixels is a STEM built database that is used in cooperation with Arlington Echo to track the health of the environment around the facility. Water quality, phytoplankton populations, and macrorganism species are all recorded by the forth grade field trips that visit the facility and learn about the Chesapeake region and its ecosystem.

Form

The form is constructed to mimic the worksheets that are filled out at the testing site and require a secure login to access. Once the submit button is pressed the form sends the information to the database, where it is added and stored.

Table

The table shows every single entry and piece of data that is stored in the database and allows for easy comparision between entries.

Graphs

Currently there are three total graphs which utilize the database to display the data in a much more visual manner. The three graphs are split up into water quality, phytoplankton populations, and macrorganism populations.

Downloads

An important goal for a database to accomplish is to have its data be usable by anyone who requests it, and the Plankton to Pixels database allows for the download of the data in both a CSV file and a sqlite3 dump.
